Prince Audu Tribute

Prince Abubakar Audu. May Allah grant you Mercy (Ameen)

Here's a timeline of Prince Abubakar Audu

  • Born on 24 October 1947
  • To Late Pa Audu Oyidi family
  • Hometown, Ogbonich Alloma in ofu Local Government Arer Kogi State
  • Attended N.A Junior Primary School Aloma
  • N.A Senior School Ankpa
  • Dennis Memorial Grammer ?School Onisha
  • Jos Commercial College
  • Studied Banking and Management in London 1975-1978
  • Fellow of the Association of International Accountants of London
  • Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Industrial Administration of Nigeria
  • The first Bank's Training officer of Africa descent
  • First black Senior Management staff of Standard Chartere Bank in London and New York
  • Executive Director of FSB International Bank Plc 1991
  • Commissinal for Finance and Economic Planning in the old Benue State 1986
  • First Bank General Manager
  • First Executive Governor of Kogi State 1992
  • Second Executive Governor of Kogi State 1999
  • Loving and caring Father, Husband, Brother and friend to all
